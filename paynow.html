Enrollment Process

  1. Step 1: Visit our website and navigate to the registration page.
  2. Step 2: Fill out the online form with your personal details.
  3. Step 3: Submit the form and wait for the confirmation email.
  4. Step 4: Complete the payment process as instructed in the email.
  5. Step 5: Receive your enrollment confirmation and start the workshop!

About Us

ACL Rehab Institute (ACLRI) is dedicated to providing top-notch rehabilitation services for individuals with ACL injuries. Our mission is to help patients recover quickly and effectively through evidence-based practices and personalized care.
